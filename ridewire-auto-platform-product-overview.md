# RideWire Auto Platform - Product Overview

## Vertical Product Scope

RideWire Auto is a comprehensive vertical solution designed specifically for automotive, motorcycle, and farm equipment service professionals. Built on the private NOVA-AI-Council-Engine, it delivers intelligent diagnostic support, training, and business operations capabilities tailored to the unique needs of vehicle service industries.

## Product Tiers

### RideWire Pro
The flagship professional edition offering:
- Full AR diagnostic capabilities with real-time visual overlays
- Complete OBD integration for all vehicle systems
- AI C-Suite decision support for complex diagnostics
- Shop management and workflow optimization
- E-commerce and parts ordering integration
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Multi-technician collaboration features

### RideWire Academy
Educational edition focused on:
- Training modules for automotive diagnostics
- Certification program integration
- Skill assessment and progress tracking
- Virtual diagnostic scenarios
- AR-guided learning experiences
- Instructor tools and classroom management

### RideWire Emergency
Mobile-first roadside assistance platform:
- Lightweight AR diagnostics for field use
- Quick OBD scan and analysis
- AI-powered triage recommendations
- GPS-enabled service routing
- Mobile parts ordering
- Customer communication tools
