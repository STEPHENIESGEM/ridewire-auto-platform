# RideWire Auto Platform

## Overview

RideWire Auto is a vertical product platform built on the private **NOVA-AI-Council-Engine**, delivering advanced AR diagnostics, OBD integration, and AI-powered decision support for automotive, motorcycle, and farm equipment professionals.

## Core Use Case

RideWire Auto combines:
- **Augmented Reality (AR) Diagnostics**: Real-time visual overlays for vehicle inspection and repair guidance
- **OBD Integration**: Direct vehicle diagnostic data capture and analysis
- **AI C-Suite**: Multi-agent AI decision support powered by NOVA core engine for expert recommendations

## Platform Modules

The platform includes integrated modules for:
- **Training & Academy**: Skill development and certification programs
- **Shop Management**: Workflow optimization and operations management
- **E-Commerce**: Parts marketplace with dropship integrations

## Product Tiers

- **RideWire Pro**: Full-featured professional platform
- **RideWire Academy**: Training-focused edition for education
- **RideWire Emergency**: Mobile-first roadside assistance solution

## Supported Verticals

- Automotive repair and diagnostics
- Motorcycle service and maintenance  
- Farm equipment and heavy machinery
