**We implement industry-standard security:**

**Technical Safeguards:**
- TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Secure authentication with password hashing
- Multi-factor authentication (available)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Administrative Safeguards:**
- Employee access controls and training
- Background checks for personnel with data access
- Incident response procedures
- Security awareness programs

**Physical Safeguards:**
- Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
- Access logging and audit trails
- Backup and disaster recovery systems

### 5.2 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may affect your personal information:
- We will notify you within 72 hours of discovery
- Notification will include nature of breach and data affected
- We will provide guidance on protective measures
- We will report to relevant authorities as required by law

### 5.3 Your Security Responsibilities

**To protect your account:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep your login credentials confidential
- Log out after using shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Periods

**We retain your data as follows:**

**Account Data:**
- Active accounts: Duration of subscription plus 30 days
- Closed accounts: 90 days (unless legal retention required)

**Diagnostic Data:**
- Raw diagnostic submissions: 2 years
- Anonymized diagnostic data: Indefinitely (for AI training)
- Analysis results in your account: Duration of subscription

**Payment Records:**
- Transaction history: 7 years (tax and accounting requirements)
- Payment methods: Until you remove them or account closes

**Communications:**
- Support tickets: 3 years
- Marketing communications: Until you unsubscribe

### 6.2 Data Deletion

**You may request deletion of:**
- Your account and personal information
- Specific diagnostic submissions
- Historical data (subject to legal retention)

**Note:** Anonymized data used for AI training cannot be deleted as it's no longer identifiable to you.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 General Rights

**You have the right to:**
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent for data processing

### 7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

**If you're in the European Economic Area:**
- All rights in Section 7.1 apply
- Right to lodge complaint with supervisory authority
- Right to object to automated decision-making
- Data Protection Officer contact: privacy@ridewire.com

### 7.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

**California residents have the right to:**
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Access personal information
- Request deletion of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

**CCPA Disclosures:**
- Categories of data collected: See Section 2
- Sources of data: Direct from you, automatically, third parties
- Business purposes: See Section 3
- Categories of third parties we share with: See Section 4.2

### 7.4 Exercising Your Rights

**To exercise any privacy right:**
- Email: privacy@ridewire.com
- Account Dashboard: Settings → Privacy → Requests
- Response time: 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests)
- Verification required to prevent unauthorized access
