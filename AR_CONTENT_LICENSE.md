## Appendix A: Technical Specifications

### File Formats Supported
- **3D Models:** FBX, GLTF, GLB, OBJ
- **Textures:** PNG, JPG (4K max resolution)
- **Animations:** Embedded in model files
- **Metadata:** JSON

### Optimization Guidelines
- **Polygon Limit:** 50K triangles (desktop), 20K (mobile)
- **Texture Size:** 2K preferred, 4K maximum
- **File Size:** 50MB maximum per asset
- **LOD Levels:** Minimum 3 levels recommended

### Content Categories
- Engine components
- Transmission systems
- Brake systems
- Electrical components
- Body panels and trim
- Interior components
- Suspension systems
- Diagnostic overlays
